## 💎 Professional Audiophile Experience
HOA Music Player Pro is a Material-You designed player featuring a **Mastering-Grade Audio Engine**. Unlike standard players, it uses **32-bit Floating Point DSP Modeling** to simulate high-end analog hardware and complex psychoacoustic environments for a truly "Physical" music experience.

## 🚀 Advanced Holophonic Audio Engine (v2)
Our custom-built DSP suite transforms your mobile device into a professional studio signal path:

- **Holophonic 6-Tap 3D Matrix**: A true 360-degree binaural engine using 6 independent virtual reflections (11ms, 19ms, 29ms, 41ms, 53ms, 67ms). This creates a massive, high-resolution acoustic pocket for every instrument, making guitars, pianos, and drums feel physically present in a 3D space.
- **Kinetic Bass Transient Shaper**: A high-speed envelope follower that detects the "hit" of a drum or the "pluck" of a bass string and dynamically boosts its attack. This creates a physically **Strong** and impactful low-end without making the sound muddy.
- **Dual-Band Sub-Bass Logic**: Independently processes Deep Bass (90Hz) and Sub-Bass (45Hz). This allows for massive "thump" while keeping the melodic bass lines crystal clear.
- **Acoustic Pre-Delay Engine**: Implements a 35ms isolation buffer. This ensures the **Core Presence** of instruments hits your ears with 100% detail *before* the ambient reflections wrap around you, preventing "washy" or "blurred" sound.
- **Mastering-Grade MS-Widening**: Intelligent Stereo expansion that forces Bass (below 200Hz) to remain in a rock-solid Mono center while aggressively widening the high-end "air" far beyond the physical limits of your headphones.
- **Binaural Crossfeed (Studio Monitor Simulation)**: Gently blends channels with a 300μs delay to simulate listening to **$50,000 Studio Monitors** in a real room, significantly reducing headphone fatigue for long listening sessions.
- **Vacuum Tube Saturator & Harmonic Exciter**: Generates "Pentode Tube" style harmonics above 5kHz to add silky detail and "expensive" texture to vocals and strings.

- **Universal Bit-Depth Support**: Natively handles both **16-bit Integer** and **32-bit Float** audio. Our engine auto-detects your device's capability to ensure pro-grade effects work on every phone.

## ❓ FAQ

### 🎧 Audio & Effects
**Q: How do I enable the High-Res Audio Engine?**
A: Go to **Settings > Audio** and toggle "High-Res Audio Engine". (Note: High-Res toggle requires app restart). Our engine now works perfectly even if this is off, thanks to our new Universal PCM support.

**Q: Why does the 3D Spatial mode sound so different?**
A: It's not a simple panner. It's a **Binaural Holophonic Matrix** that uses time delays and phase shifts to trick your brain into hearing sound from specific physical directions.

**Q: What about the Equalizer?**
A: We use professional DSP effects instead of a standard EQ:
- **Stereo Balance & Width**
- **Diamond Clarity** (Vocal & Air enhancement)
- **Kinetic Bass Boost** (Analog-style with punch detection)
- **Room Effect** (Advanced Reverb with Pre-Delay)

### 📂 Library & Files
**Q: Why aren't my songs showing up?**
- Check **Settings > Other > Filter song duration** (set to 0).
- Try **Settings > Library > Rescan**.
- Ensure files are in a supported Android format (MP3, FLAC, M4A, WAV, etc.).

**Q: Why are random pictures showing as album art?**
A: Enable **Settings > Images > Ignore media store covers**.

**Q: How do I use offline synced lyrics?**
A: HOA supports `.lrc` files. Place them in `/sdcard/HOAMusic/lyrics/` named as `Song - Artist.lrc` or use the built-in lyrics editor.

### 🎨 Customization & Support
**Q: How do I change the theme?**
A: **Settings > Look and feel > Select theme**. We support Material You and custom accent colors.

**Q: How do I restore my purchases?**
A: Ensure you are logged into the same Google Account used for the purchase in the Play Store before installing.

**Q: The app is crashing or behaving strangely?**
A: Try clearing the app cache/data or reinstalling from the Play Store. Changing the theme back and forth can also fix some UI glitches.
